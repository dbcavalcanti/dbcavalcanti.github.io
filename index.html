About

I am a researcher working on numerical modeling of coupled hydromechanical processes in fractured porous media, with a focus on embedded finite element formulations and open-source scientific software.

Research interests

  • Finite element methods for fractured porous media
  • Hydromechanical and two-phase flow coupling
  • Embedded / strong-discontinuity formulations (E-FEM, SDA)
  • Open-source FEM frameworks and scientific computing
